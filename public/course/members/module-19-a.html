The Sign Keys

Every sign (+, -, x, ÷) is like a Key. If you have two numbers and a target result, only one sign will unlock the door. Instead of guessing, we look at the target. Is the result big? Use a "Growth" key (+ or x). Is it small? Use a "Shrink" key (- or ÷).

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Linear Story" Trap: Most children read left-to-right. For $2 + 3^2$, they do $2+3=5$, then $5^2=25$. This fails in the 11+.

The Fix: The "Heavyweight" Rule. Roots and Powers are heaviest. In the example, the power happens first ($3^2=9$), making the weight $2+9=11$. Find the heaviest sign first!
