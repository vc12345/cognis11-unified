The Orientation Trap "Does the arrow point toward the Star or away from it after a 180° flip?"

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Mirage Neighbor" Trap: Distractors often use the correct symbols but place them in impossible spots. They might show two faces touching that you know are actually Opposites (like the front and back of the box).

The Insider Fix: The "Law of Opposites." Tell your child: "Opposites are shy." If Symbol A and Symbol B are on opposite sides of the cube, they can never be seen at the same time. If a multiple-choice answer shows both, it’s a lie. Cross it out immediately and don't waste another second checking its rotation. Opposites can never be neighbors.
