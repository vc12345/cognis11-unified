Phase 4: Common Mistake to Watch For

Phase 4: Common Mistake to Watch For

The "Straight Line" Trap: Most children naturally calculate everything from left to right. Faced with an expression like 2 + 32, they often do 2 + 3 = 5 first, and then square it to get 32 = 25. This mistake will lose points in competitive exams.

The Easy Fix: The "Heavyweight Leader" Rule. Roots and Powers carry the heaviest weight. In this example, the power must be solved first (32 = 9), changing our calculation path to 2 + 9 = 11. Always hunt down and solve your heaviest symbols first!
