Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Linear Story" Trap: Most children read missing sign problems like a book—left to right. If they see $2 + 3^2$, they often do $2 + 3 = 5$, then $5^2 = 25$. This "storytelling" math will fail every single time in the 11+.

The Fix: The "Heavyweight" First Rule. Tell your child: "BODMAS is a gravity rule." Roots and Powers are the heaviest; they must be solved before addition or subtraction can even move. In the example above, the power happens first ($3^2 = 9$), making the true weight $2 + 9 = 11$. Find the heaviest sign first, then balance the rest.
