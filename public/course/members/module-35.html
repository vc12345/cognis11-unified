Module 35: Word Morphing

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Think of this as the "Stolen Letter" mystery. In the 11+, your child will be shown two words and told that exactly one letter has moved from the first word into the second. The goal is to create two entirely new, valid words. The secret weapon is recognizing that the letter you move must be "expendable"—it must leave a perfect word behind while creating a perfect word at its destination.

The Easy Swap "Move one letter from CHART to EAT to make two new words."
The Hidden Slot "One letter can be moved from STEAM to BIT. Where does it land?"
The 11+ Challenge "Find the letter that migrates from POUND to EACH. Both results must be real words."

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "One-Sided" Trap: Most children find a letter that makes a great new word in the second box (e.g., taking the 'H' from CHART to make HEAT) and stop there. They forget to check if the word they left behind (CART) is actually a real word!

The Insider Fix: The "Dual Dictionary" Rule. Tell your child: "The letter is a bridge—if one side collapses, the answer is wrong." They must perform two separate checks: 1. Is the first word still alive? 2. Is the second word newly born? If they don't see two real words, they must put the letter back and try another one. Both words must pass the test.
