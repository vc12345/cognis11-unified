Phase 4: Common Mistake to Watch For

The "Single-Track Autopilot" Speed Trap: Children naturally slip into a repetitive calculation rhythm. If they successfully move the first few consonants backward by -1 space, they will often accidentally apply that same -1 rule to the upcoming vowels without thinking.

The Easy Fix: The "Label Before You Leap" Rule. Remind your child: "Never rush into the math blindly." Write a small, clear 'V' or 'C' directly above every letter in the question word before transforming anything. Tagging the letters stops autopilot mistakes from breaking the cipher pattern.
