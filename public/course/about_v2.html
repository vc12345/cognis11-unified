The Origin Story

The human entry point.

Cognis11 exists because the most capable children are often the most poorly served by standard 11+ preparation — and the reason is identifiable and fixable.

"The preparation isn't failing because the child isn't working hard enough. It's failing because it was designed for a different type of mind."

Standard 11+ prep focuses on volume and pattern recognition. But for the child who constructs understanding before acting, high-volume drilling often leads to frustration and speed plateaus. They are reasoners being asked to behave like calculators.

We adapt Schema-Based Instruction (SBI) — a pillar of cognitive psychology — to provide these children with the structural "X-Ray" vision they need to classify problem structures in seconds. We help them move from re-deriving logic to recognizing it.

The Parent-Led Mandate

A child does not build a new mental model from passive exposure to a screen; they build it through interaction with a trusted adult who can read the moment of confusion.

Cognis11 is designed to be led by you. We provide the 60-second parent prep and the interactive tools. You provide the 10 minutes of shared dialogue that makes the concept stick.

The Founding Team

Our team brings together academia (cognitive neuroscience), industry analysis, and digital product building. We pointed these backgrounds at the 11+ curriculum to build a better entry point for students who prioritize understanding over memorization.
